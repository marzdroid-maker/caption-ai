Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 22, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide our service:

  • Account Data: We store your email address, subscription status, and usage count in our secure database (MongoDB).
  • Affiliate Data: If you join as an affiliate, we store your Stripe Connect ID to process payouts.
  • Usage Data: We generate captions using AI. We do not permanently store the text of your generated captions.

2. How We Store Data

  • Database: Your account details are stored securely in MongoDB Atlas.
  • Local Storage: We use your browser's "Local Storage" to remember your session so you don't have to log in every time. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.

3. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following trusted providers solely for functionality:

  • Stripe: For payment processing and affiliate payouts. We never see or store your credit card details.
  • Groq AI: Your input text is sent to Groq to generate captions. Groq does not use your data to train their models.

4. Your Rights

We do not sell your data. You may request the deletion of your account and data at any time by contacting us.
